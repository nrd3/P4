About us


Hope for Humanity is a nonprofit organization intended to spread awareness for preventable atrocities happening around the world. By doing this, we can end suffering from poverty, genocide, and injustice. If we all stand united against the oppressors we will succeed.


Mission


Our mission is to encourage those able to donate and/or volunteer their time to make a difference. It is important to remind the world that we are all humans and are created equal and that those who are privileged have a real opportunity to promote change and a better world.
